HAPPY WOMEN'S DAY

International Women’s Day is an annual event that celebrates the amazing achievements of women throughout history.
It’s a day dedicated to highlighting the myriad ways that women have influenced, and continue to influence, society and the world at large.
As well as celebrating the contributions of incredible women, International Women’s Day is also a day to highlight and raise awareness of inequalities and other issues that women still face today.
Historically, women have been variously denied their right to vote, work, seek an education and have financial independence, among many other basic human rights.
Because of the brilliant women who fought against these injustices, women today have far more say in the world now than they have in the past.
There are, however, still challenges that women face every day. Gender equality, reproductive rights, and violence and abuse against women are still pertinent issues.
International Women’s Day is a chance to fight for these challenges to be addressed.
